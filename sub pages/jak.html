Jak and Daxter

Jak and Daxter: The Precursor Legacy is a platforming adventure game for the PlayStation 2 that follows the journey of Jak and his sidekick Daxter.
After a mishap involving dark eco, Daxter is transformed into an ottsel, and the duo sets out to discover the truth behind the mysterious Precursor artifacts while battling various enemies and solving puzzles.
With its vibrant open world, engaging gameplay mechanics, and charming character interactions, the game is celebrated for laying the foundation for a beloved franchise.
