Grand Turismo 4

Gran Turismo 4 is a racing simulation video game for the PlayStation 2, celebrated for its realistic driving physics and extensive car collection.
Players can choose from over 700 vehicles and compete on more than 50 tracks worldwide, including real-life locations and fictional circuits.
The game features a robust career mode, detailed tuning options, and stunning graphics that create an immersive racing experience, making it a standout title in the Gran Turismo series.
