SSX Tricky

SSX Tricky is an exhilarating snowboarding video game for the PlayStation 2 that combines high-speed racing with stylish tricks and stunts.
Players can choose from a diverse cast of characters, each with unique abilities, as they navigate creatively designed courses set in stunning mountain environments.
The game features an iconic "Tricky" meter that allows players to unleash spectacular tricks and gain boosts, enhancing the competitive and fun atmosphere of this beloved winter sports title.
