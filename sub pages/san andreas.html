San Andreas Grand Theft Auto

Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas is an action-adventure video game set in the fictional state of San Andreas, which includes three major cities: Los Santos, San Fierro, and Las Venturas.
Players control Carl "CJ" Johnson, who returns to his hometown to uncover the truth behind his mother's murder while navigating gang warfare, corruption, and a richly detailed open world.
The game is renowned for its immersive storytelling, expansive environment, and diverse gameplay mechanics, including vehicle customization and a wide range of side missions.
