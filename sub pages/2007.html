SmackDown vs. Raw 2007

SmackDown vs. Raw 2007 on PS2 is a professional wrestling video game that features an extensive roster of WWE Superstars and Legends, allowing players to experience the thrill of wrestling matches and rivalries.
The game introduces the new "Fight for Your Moment" mode, where players create their own wrestler and navigate a storyline filled with decisions that impact their career.
With enhanced graphics, improved gameplay mechanics, and various match types, including the popular Royal Rumble and Ladder matches, it offers an engaging experience for wrestling fans.
