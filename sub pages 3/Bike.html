Excite Bike

Excite Bike is a classic motocross racing game for the NES, where players race against time and opponents on challenging tracks filled with jumps, obstacles, and hazards.
The game allows players to control the speed and balance of their bike, with a focus on avoiding overheating and strategically using ramps to gain speed.
With its simple yet addictive game play and a track editor that lets players create their own courses, Excite Bike became one of the standout titles in early NES history.
