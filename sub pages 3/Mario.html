Super Mario Bros. 3

Super Mario Bros. 3 is a platforming masterpiece for the NES, where players control Mario and Luigi as they embark on a quest to rescue Princess Peach and the Mushroom Kingdom from Bowser and his mischievous Koopalings.
The game introduces innovative power-ups like the Super Leaf, which gives Mario the ability to fly, and features diverse levels spread across eight unique worlds filled with secrets, enemies, and challenging obstacles.
Widely regarded as one of the greatest video games of all time, Super Mario Bros. 3 set a new standard for creativity, gameplay, and design in the platforming genre.
