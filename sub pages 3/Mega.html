Mega Man 2

Mega Man 2 is a beloved action-platformer for the NES, where players control the heroic robot Mega Man as he battles through stages filled with enemies and bosses created by the evil Dr. Wily.
Each stage is themed around a unique Robot Master, and upon defeating them, Mega Man gains their special weapon, which can be used to strategize in future battles.
Known for its challenging gameplay, iconic music, and tight controls, Mega Man 2 is often hailed as one of the greatest games in the Mega Man series and a classic of the NES era.
