Punch-Out!!

Punch-Out!! is a boxing game for the NES where players control the underdog boxer, Little Mac, as he fights his way through a series of quirky and challenging opponents in pursuit of the championship title.
Each opponent has distinct patterns and weaknesses that players must learn to exploit through well-timed dodges, punches, and counterattacks.
Known for its fast-paced gameplay, memorable characters, and iconic fights, Punch-Out!! is a classic in the sports genre and remains one of the NES's most enduring titles.
