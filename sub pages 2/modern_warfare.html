Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare

Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare is a first-person shooter that shifts the series from World War II settings to contemporary military conflicts.
Players engage in intense combat missions as members of elite military units, such as the U.S. Marine Corps and British SAS, in a storyline centered around global terrorism and nuclear threats.
Known for its groundbreaking multiplayer modes and cinematic single-player campaign, Modern Warfare set a new standard for the Call of Duty franchise and the FPS genre.
