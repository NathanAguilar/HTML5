Call of Duty Black Ops

Call of Duty: Black Ops is a first-person shooter set during the Cold War era, focusing on secret missions and covert operations.
Players take on the role of various operatives, including Alex Mason, as they unravel a complex conspiracy involving brainwashing and covert warfare.
Known for its intense campaign, iconic multiplayer modes, and the introduction of the popular Zombies mode, Black Ops became a standout entry in the Call of Duty franchise.
