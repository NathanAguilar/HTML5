Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V is an open-world action-adventure game set in the sprawling city of Los Santos and its surrounding areas, blending elements of crime, exploration, and storytelling.
Players control three distinct characters—Michael, Franklin, and Trevor—each with their own unique abilities and story arcs, as they navigate a world filled with heists, criminal activities, and a satirical take on modern life.
With its rich narrative, expansive multiplayer mode, and detailed environment, GTA V has become one of the best-selling video games of all time, receiving widespread acclaim for its gameplay and design.
