Last of Us

The Last of Us is an action-adventure game set in a post-apocalyptic world ravaged by a fungal infection that turns humans into deadly creatures.
Players follow Joel, a hardened survivor, as he escorts a young girl named Ellie across the United States, discovering along the way that she may hold the key to humanity's survival.
Praised for its gripping narrative, emotional depth, and immersive gameplay, The Last of Us is considered one of the most critically acclaimed games of its generation.
